## 1) Value cannot be NULL

### FACULTY
- `FACULTY_SHORT_NAME`
- `FACULTY_NAME`

### SUBJECT
- `SUBJECT_NR`
- `SUBJECT_SEMESTER_LVL`
- `DEGREE_SHORT_NAME`
- `SUBJECT_NAME`
- `SUBJECT_ELECTIVE`
- `SUBJECT_CURRICULUM`
- `SUBJECT_SCHEDULE`
- (`SUBJECT_COMMENT` may be NULL)

### ACADEMIC_STAFF
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ID`
- `FIRST_NAME`
- `LAST_NAME`
- `FACULTY_SHORT_NAME`
- `IS_PROF`
- (`ACADEMIC_STAFF_COMMENT` may be NULL)

### ACADEMIC_STAFF_WORKLOAD
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ID`
- `SEMESTER_NR`
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_REDUCTION`
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ROLE`

### DEGREE_PROGRAM
- `DEGREE_SHORT_NAME`
- `DEGREE_NAME`
- `FACULTY_SHORT_NAME`

### LECTURER
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ID`
- (`SUPERVISOR` may be NULL)

### COURSE
- `SUBJECT_NR`
- `DEGREE_SHORT_NAME`
- (`COURSE_COMMENT` may be NULL)

### COURSE_ASSIGNMENT
- `COURSE_ASSIGNMENT_TERM`
- `SUBJECT_NR`
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ID`
- `DEGREE_SHORT_NAME`
- (`TEACHING_HOURS` may be NULL)

---

## 2) Value must be INTEGER 

### SUBJECT
- `SUBJECT_SEMESTER_LVL` (BIGINT)
- `SUBJECT_CURRICULUM` (BIGINT)
- `SUBJECT_SCHEDULE` (BIGINT)

### ACADEMIC_STAFF
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ID` (BIGINT)

### ACADEMIC_STAFF_WORKLOAD
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ID` (BIGINT)
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_REDUCTION` (DECIMAL(5,2) — numeric, not integer)

### LECTURER
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ID` (BIGINT)
- `SUPERVISOR` (BIGINT, nullable)

### COURSE_ASSIGNMENT
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_ID` (BIGINT)
- `TEACHING_HOURS` (DECIMAL(5,2) — numeric, not integer)

### FACULTY / DEGREE_PROGRAM / COURSE / SUBJECT identifiers (not integers)
- `FACULTY_SHORT_NAME` (CHAR)
- `DEGREE_SHORT_NAME` (CHAR)
- `SUBJECT_NR` (VARCHAR)
- `SEMESTER_NR` / `COURSE_ASSIGNMENT_TERM` (VARCHAR/CHAR)

---

## Additional constraint-based business rules 

### SUBJECT
- `SUBJECT_ELECTIVE` must be one of: `P`, `W`, `Z`
- `SUBJECT_CURRICULUM >= 0` and `SUBJECT_SCHEDULE >= 0`

### ACADEMIC_STAFF_WORKLOAD
- `ACADEMIC_STAFF_REDUCTION >= 0`

### COURSE_ASSIGNMENT
- `TEACHING_HOURS >= 0` (if not NULL)
- `COURSE_ASSIGNMENT_TERM` must start with `WS` or `SS`
